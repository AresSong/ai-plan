UX Concept Design SOP — Role Swimlanes

Each block follows Action · Input → Output. “N/A” means no standalone deliverable is expected; the role may still be consulted. Double arrows identify the recurring two-way collaboration needed at that step.
BA / PMUXUI / FrontendBackend↕ two-way collaboration

Does every role act at every step?

No. The diagram names a role only where it owns work or must provide a material review. In a greenfield product, UI/Frontend discovery may be N/A; in a small team, one person may cover several roles.

What “UI signals” means

That wording has been removed. The relevant input is an existing UI audit: current components, accessibility debt, platform limitations, and user-visible patterns. It is not a separate UX-to-UI signal.

How to use this SOP

At each step, run a short cross-functional review around the stated output. Do not move to build readiness until user desirability, business value, UI feasibility, and backend feasibility are sufficiently evidenced.
